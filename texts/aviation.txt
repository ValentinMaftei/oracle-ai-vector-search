Aviation refers to the activities surrounding mechanical flight and the aircraft industry. Airplanes were first developed in the early twentieth century. The Wright brothers made the first controlled and sustained flight of a powered aircraft in nineteen hundred and three. Since that time aviation has expanded to touch almost every part of global society. Modern jet engines allow people to travel across oceans in just a few hours. Pilots use complex navigation systems to stay on course during bad weather. The aerospace industry continues to innovate with electric planes and faster supersonic flight. Safe air travel depends on strict air traffic control and regular maintenance of the machines. Engineers work hard to make wings more efficient and engines much quieter for people living near airports. Commercial airlines carry millions of passengers every single day to destinations all around the world.
